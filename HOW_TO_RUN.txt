===================================================
  DEAL PIPELINE — Stuart Portfolio Consultants
  HOW TO RUN  (Zero Install)
===================================================

HOW TO OPEN THE APP:
---------------------
1. Double-click  "Deal Pipeline App.html"
2. It opens in your browser (Chrome or Edge)
3. That's it — no install, no setup needed!


FEATURES:
----------
  Dashboard   — Stats, overdue alerts, pipeline chart
  All Deals   — Searchable table with every deal field
  Kanban      — Visual board across all 5 stages
  + Add Deal  — Button top-right to add a new deal
  ✏️  Edit    — Edit any deal
  →  Advance  — Move deal to next stage in one click
  📧 Email    — Generate a follow-up email to copy/send
  🗑 Delete   — Remove a deal (asks you to confirm)
  Export CSV  — Download all your deals as a spreadsheet


YOUR DATA:
-----------
  - Saves automatically in your browser (no files to manage)
  - Use "Export CSV" regularly to back up your data
  - If you clear your browser history/data, deals may be lost
    — so export to CSV as your backup!
  - Your data stays on your computer — never sent anywhere


THE 5 PIPELINE STAGES:
------------------------
  Outreach → Meeting → Due Diligence → Negotiation → Closed


IMPORTANT NOTES:
-----------------
  - Keep the browser tab open while using the app
  - Works in Chrome and Edge (recommended)
  - You need internet the first time (to load React/Tailwind)
    After that it works offline too


COMING LATER (optional upgrade):
----------------------------------
